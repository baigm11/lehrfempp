template<typename SCALAR>
class lf::fe::LinearLagrangianFESpace< SCALAR >

Linear Lagrangian Finite Element space.

Just a specialization of UniformScalarFiniteElementSpace based on TriaLinearLagrangeFE, QuadLinearLagrangeFE.

Just a specialization of fe_space_uniform_scalar based on TriaLinearLagrangeFE, QuadLinearLagrangeFE.
